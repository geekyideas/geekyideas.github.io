About

We are a group of students from Sangamner college, Sangamner who have taken up an initiative to provide sorted information and knowledge to the young rural minds.
We have come up with a e-magazine, GeekyIdeas specially for the students of computer science.
The magazine features latest trends, useful apps and websites, inspirational stories, facts, quiz and many more interesting stuff.
We believe that students from rural area carries a lot of potential. And just a little bit of exposure can push them towards a successful future.
